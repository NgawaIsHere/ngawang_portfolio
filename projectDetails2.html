Project Overview

The Product

E-Iteria is a local restaurant specializing in fast food and custom cakes for special occasions like birthdays and celebrations. Since cakes and certain menu items take time to prepare, E-Iteria needs a pre-ordering app that allows customers to place their orders in advance, ensuring timely preparation and pickup.

Project information

  • Category: Mobile Application
  • Project Duration: Aug - Nov 2023
View on Figma

The Problem Statement

The current ordering process at E-Iteria is inefficient for customers who need food or custom cakes ready at a specific time. Without a pre-order system, customers face long wait times, last-minute rushes, and uncertainty about availability, leading to frustration and missed opportunities for celebrations.

The Goal

Develop a user-friendly pre-ordering app for E-Iteria that allows customers to schedule their fast food and special occasion cake orders in advance. This will enable the restaurant to manage preparation times efficiently while ensuring customers receive their orders fresh and on time for their celebrations.

Accessibility Considerations

  1. Used clear icons and consistent layouts to support easy navigation for all users, including first-time app users and those with limited tech experience.
  2. Included tooltips and visual cues to explain customization options, helping users unfamiliar with food or cake ordering terms.
  3. Enabled online pre-ordering and notifications so users with mobility or time constraints can avoid physical queues and missed pickups.
  4. Ensured high text contrast, readable fonts, and scalable interface elements for better visibility and usability across different devices and for users with low vision.
  5. Planned a simple introductory walkthrough to guide new users through key features and reduce confusion during their first use.
